King of the Hill

In King of the Hill, players must control a specific area of the map for as long as possible. The longer you hold the hill, the more points your team earns.

How to Play

  • Control the designated hill area to earn points for your team.
  • Defend the hill from enemy players who are trying to take control.
  • The team with the most points at the end wins.
