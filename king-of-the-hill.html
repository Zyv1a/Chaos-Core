King of the Hill

In King of the Hill, players compete to capture and hold a central hill point. The longer your team controls the hill, the more points you earn. The team with the most points at the end of the match wins.

How to Play

  • Capture the hill in the center of the map.
  • Hold the hill as long as possible to earn points.
  • Defend the hill from enemy players trying to take control.

Tips

  • Coordinate with your team to hold the hill effectively.
  • Use high-ground positions to get a better view of enemies.
