Team Deathmatch

Team Deathmatch is a classic mode where two teams compete to eliminate each other. The first team to reach the target number of kills wins.

How to Play

  • Work with your team to eliminate the enemy team.
  • The team that reaches the kill target first wins.
  • Strategize with your teammates to ensure victory.
