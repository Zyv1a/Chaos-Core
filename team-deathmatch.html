Team Deathmatch

In Team Deathmatch, two teams compete to eliminate each other. The first team to reach a certain number of kills wins the match.

How to Play

  • Work together with your team to eliminate the opposing team.
  • Respawn after death and continue fighting.
  • Earn points by eliminating enemies.

Tips

  • Coordinate with teammates for effective strategies.
  • Use cover and be strategic when engaging enemies.
