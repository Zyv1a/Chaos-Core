Free-for-All

In Free-for-All, every player is for themselves. The goal is to eliminate as many opponents as possible and rack up the highest score. The player with the most kills at the end wins!

How to Play

  • Every player fights alone.
  • Eliminate other players to earn points.
  • The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Tips

  • Stay mobile and unpredictable to avoid being an easy target.
  • Pick your fights wisely and avoid unnecessary confrontations.
