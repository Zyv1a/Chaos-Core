Free-for-All

In Free-for-All mode, every player is on their own. The goal is simple: eliminate everyone else before they eliminate you!

How to Play

  • Eliminate every other player on the map.
  • The player with the most kills when the timer runs out wins.
  • Be fast and strategic to outplay others.
