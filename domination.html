Domination

In Domination, teams fight to capture and hold strategic points on the map. The more points your team controls, the more points you earn. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.

How to Play

  • Capture control points spread across the map.
  • Hold the points to earn points for your team.
  • Defend captured points from the enemy team.

Tips

  • Focus on holding multiple points at once.
  • Prioritize defense on high-value control points.
