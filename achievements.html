Achievements

In Chaos Core, you can earn various achievements by completing in-game challenges. These achievements will showcase your skills and progress, unlocking rewards and bragging rights!

List of Achievements

How Achievements Work

  • Achievements are unlocked by completing specific challenges in the game.
  • They can be tracked through your player profile.
  • Some achievements unlock in-game rewards, such as skins, badges, or XP boosts.
  • Keep an eye on the leaderboard to see who has unlocked the most achievements!
