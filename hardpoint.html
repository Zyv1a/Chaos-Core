Hardpoint

In Hardpoint, teams fight to control rotating hardpoints on the map. The longer your team controls a hardpoint, the more points you earn. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins!

How to Play

  • Capture and hold hardpoints as they rotate around the map.
  • Protect the hardpoint from enemy teams.
  • The team that controls the hardpoint earns points over time.

Tips

  • Coordinate with your team to defend hardpoints effectively.
  • Stay mobile and be ready for the next rotating point.
