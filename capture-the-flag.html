Capture the Flag

In this mode, two teams fight to capture the opposing team's flag and return it to their base. The first team to capture the flag a set number of times wins the match.

How to Play

  • Steal the enemy flag while defending your own.
  • Work together with your team to prevent the enemy from capturing your flag.
  • Score points by returning the flag to your base.

Tips

  • Coordinate with your team for both offense and defense.
  • Use vehicles to quickly move across the map.
