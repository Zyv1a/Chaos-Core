Capture the Flag

In Capture the Flag, the goal is simple: capture the enemy team's flag and bring it back to your base while defending your own flag from being captured.

How to Play

  • Capture the enemy's flag by entering their base.
  • Bring the flag back to your base without being killed.
  • Defend your own flag to prevent the enemy from capturing it.
  • The first team to a certain number of captures wins.

Strategy Tips

  • Work with your team to guard your flag while sending players to capture the enemy's flag.
  • Communicate well with your team for coordinated attacks and defenses.
  • Use speed and stealth to infiltrate the enemy base and avoid detection.
