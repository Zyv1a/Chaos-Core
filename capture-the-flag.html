Capture the Flag

In this mode, your goal is to capture the enemy’s flag and return it to your base, while defending your own flag from being captured. Teamwork is key!

How to Play

  • Capture the enemy’s flag and return it to your base.
  • Protect your flag from being captured by the enemy team.
  • Work as a team to strategize your offense and defense.
